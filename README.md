# 🏠 Mom Is Coming Home

**A frantic, top-down "clean-em-up" comedy game built in just 1 hour.**

Mom just texted: **"I'm 5 minutes away."** The house is a disaster, your sibling just spilled Milo, and the AI Home Assistant is mocking your "skill issues." Can you clean the house before the slipper gets equipped?

---

## 🏆 Hackathon Achievement
This project was developed within a strict **60-minute timeframe** using Google AI Studio and web app tools. It secured **3rd Place** by combining relatable stress with "rage-bait" comedic difficulty.

## 🎮 Game Features
*   **Three Rooms of Chaos:** Navigate the Living Room, Kitchen, and Bedroom.
*   **The Mess Hierarchy:** 
    *   **Dishes** to the sink, **Clothes** to the laundry, and **Trash** to the bin.
    *   **Failed Exam Papers** must be hidden or shredded immediately.
    *   **Spilled Milo** requires a mop before Mom sees the stains.
*   **Rage-Comedy Mechanics:**
    *   **Passive-Aggressive AI:** Receieve "helpful" advice like *"I never said that"* or *"According to my analysis, this is your fault"*.
    *   **Random Disasters:** Handle cat-induced vase breaks and loud rice cookers that spike your suspicion meter.
    *   **The Inspection Report:** Face the final judgment with ranks from **S-Rank (Good Child)** to **F-Rank (You are cooked)**.

## 🕹️ Controls
*   **Move:** `WASD` or `Arrow Keys`.
*   **Interact:** `E` or `Click` near objects to clean, hide, or move them.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
*   **Architecture:** 100% Single-file HTML/CSS/JS for maximum portability.
*   **Graphics:** Lightweight UI using CSS shapes and emoji-based icons—no external assets required.
*   **Systems:** Integrated Countdown Timer, Cleanliness Score, Suspicion Meter, and Rage Meter.
